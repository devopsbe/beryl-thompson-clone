A JOURNEY TO THE SELF

TRUST WHAT YOU HEAR WHEN YOU LISTEN

ABOUT THAT JOURNEY ...

Begin From Within!

The Journey to the Self is a personal system of self-management that allows anyone who is intentional to shift the energy of their experiences from discord to harmony through trusting what you hear when you listen. I believe this to be our birthright as humans being.

It is said that the longest distance any of us will ever travel is the distance from the head to the heart. I call this the Journey to the Self … the authentic, essence Self … the True Self. It's clear to me that that Journey is the real purpose for life, the underlying reason that each of us agreed to be here. For me, it's the only perspective that works. Consistently, once life is perceived as that journey, answers to my questions fall into place.

In childhood we are entrained to the "rules" of the physical world and to process through the ego/intellect. Our adult lives are consistently filled with changes chosen to shift us from that original indoctrination. For me, it was pivotal to learn how to process through the heart center with an emphasis on the original, valid feelings from my Child Self. That lens shift and subsequent fulfillment has changed the trajectory of my life. What was not working, consistently leaving me in frustration and ill health, now balances within my purview of co-creation. In 25 years of private practice it's a process that has worked for each one who has chosen to apply it.

PROCESS

Through regular guidance that reflects back to the Self and then practice, each student is actually practicing living through the heart center. Since the process is a return to the original Self, practice causes you to remember yourSelf and you realize that the new "habit" really isn't new. Since 1994, this process has worked for 100% of those who apply it.
